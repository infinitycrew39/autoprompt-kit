# Advanced Chaining Templates

## Chain A: Market Research Chain

Step 1 prompt:
Generate 20 assumptions about <MARKET>.

Step 2 prompt:
Stress-test each assumption with counterarguments and confidence scores.

Step 3 prompt:
Convert top findings into a decision memo.

## Chain B: Content Production Chain

Step 1 prompt:
Generate content pillars from <OFFER> and <AUDIENCE>.

Step 2 prompt:
Create 30 post briefs from those pillars.

Step 3 prompt:
Write 5 full posts and 5 short-form variants.

## Chain C: Coding Delivery Chain

Step 1 prompt:
Break <FEATURE_REQUEST> into tasks and dependencies.

Step 2 prompt:
Generate implementation draft for highest-impact task.

Step 3 prompt:
Generate tests and regression checklist.

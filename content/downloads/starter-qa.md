# Prompt QA Checklist

## Before running

- Is the goal specific and measurable?
- Is relevant context provided?
- Are constraints explicit?
- Is the output format defined?

## After running

- Did the output follow structure?
- Is anything missing or hallucinated?
- Are recommendations actionable?
- Is confidence level justified?

## Improve loop prompt
Revise your previous answer with:
1. Higher precision
2. Fewer assumptions
3. Explicit unknowns
4. Concrete next actions
